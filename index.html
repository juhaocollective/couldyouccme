Juhao Collective


started as a passion project of a group of like-minded Dutch-born Asian creatives. The collective aspires to bring people with shared interests together to collaborate, start new dialogues and form different narratives. Juhao Collective curates and produces content on Asian matters and/or work by Asian creatives.


‘could you cc me?'

is a zine that sends a carbon copy (cc) of a collaborative effort back to every participant. Just like your cc’ed e-mails, we chain you together and zip you up into a nice publication. Join us and find out what every edition brings.

[enter and exit] / edition01 / 'could you cc me?'

This edition is about the relation between ‘the private and the public’. In the current pandemic of COVID-19, we’ve seen a redefinition of these concepts. A physical shift of what used to be public, had turned deserted and a personal private space served a public purpose. Now that certain countries are relaxing their measures, we again are reconsidering what the private is in relation to the public. The civil rights movement has likewise seen a reenforcement of urgency in voicing out one’s personal opinions in the public domain. These public movements, again, influence people to educate themselves and others around them.

As we can see, a constant flux of information online and worldwide organised protests challenge ‘the private and the public’. How have the past few months changed your view on ’the private and the public’? How does it look like, sound like, feel like, taste like or even smell like?

We challenge you to express your experience with 'could you cc me?’. We’d like to receive a free form of visualisation in any medium of your choice together with

  • the title of your work,
  • a +/- 50 word explanation,
  • your medium and the following information.

  • (studio) name
  • country of residence
  • instagram handle and/or website
  • e-mail
